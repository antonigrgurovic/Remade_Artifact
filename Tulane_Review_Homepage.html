Welcome to the Tulane Review!


The Tulane Review is Tulane University's student-run literary art magazine. A bi-annual journal featuring international writers and artists, the Review celebrates all styles and techniques of prose, poetry, and art.

We are accepting submissions for the Spring 2016 issue. To learn more about how to submit, please read our submission guidelines.

Copies of the Fall 2015 issue are available on Tulane University's uptown campus in the Lavin-Bernick Center, Howard Tilton Memorial Library, Norman Mayer Building, and the Woldenberg Art Center.
